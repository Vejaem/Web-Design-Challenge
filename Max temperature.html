Max Temperature

As expected, the weather become significantly warmr as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. this may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time time of the year the data was gathered.
